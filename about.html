About Us

The Hero Xpulse 200 4V is an adventure bike with a starting price of Rs. 1,59,511. It is only available in one variant and three colours. The Hero Xpulse 200 4V is powered by a 199.6cc BS6 engine that produces 18.8 bhp and 17.35 Nm of torque. Hero Xpulse 200 4V has anti-lock braking system with both front and rear disc brakes. This Xpulse 200 4V bike weighs 158 kg and has a 13-liter fuel tank.

Now, the Xpulse 200 4V was already a significant upgrade over the 2-valve version. But we were in the hopes that Hero would come up with a rally Kit for the bike. Something that we loved in the previous version as well. In the 2-valve version, the Rally Kit came with longer-travel suspension and more off-road focused tyres and ergonomics, among other things. And now, Hero seems to have something similar in the pipeline for the 4V, but it will have a little more than before.
